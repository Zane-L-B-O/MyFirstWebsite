Top 3 Cities I've Called Home

  • Cottageville, SC
  • North Charleston, SC
  • Goose Creek, SC

I lived in Goose Creek for a good few years of my childhood but I don't remember much so I cant describe much. Cottageville is a peaceful place nice and quiet as long as the neighbors aren't hunting or throwing a party but it has a few general stores, gas stations, not that many people (both nice and mean), and good food. North Charleston is where I've gone through my entire highschool life and it's what you expect of a city, a lot of people, traffic, a bunch of gas stations, stores, resturants, and a bunch of other things you'd expect in a city except for sky scrapers.
